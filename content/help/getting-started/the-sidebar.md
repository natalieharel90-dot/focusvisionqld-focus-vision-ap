**What this is for:** Knowing what every item in the left-hand menu does, so you can go straight to the right place.

## The menu items

- **Home** — your shift summary of work waiting.
- **Patients** — the full list of patients in recovery.
- **New patients** — patients who signed up but are not yet set up.
- **Procedures** — the library of procedure templates and content.
- **Messages** — the shared inbox for patient conversations.
- **Triage** — check-ins and flags that need clinical review.
- **Analytics** — clinic-wide trends and charts.
- **Reports** — downloadable activity and compliance reports.
- **Feedback** — patient ratings and comments to acknowledge.
- **Bulk push** — send content or messages to groups of patients.
- **Audit log** — the record of every staff action.
- **Settings** — clinic profile, staff, and dashboard configuration.

A **Help** link is also available for these articles.

## Badge counts

Some menu items show a small coloured number — a **badge**. It is a live count of work waiting in that area.

- **New patients** — patients still awaiting setup.
- **Messages** — unread messages from patients.
- **Triage** — open flags plus check-ins needing review.
- **Feedback** — patient feedback not yet acknowledged.

When the count reaches zero, the badge disappears.

:::patient-sees
Nothing — this is staff-internal.
:::

:::watch-out
Some items only appear depending on your role and access level. If you cannot see **Analytics**, **Reports**, or **Audit log**, your account does not have access — ask your clinic manager.
:::
