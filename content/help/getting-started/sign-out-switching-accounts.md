**What this is for:** Signing out at the end of a shift, and handing a shared computer to the next staff member safely.

## How to sign out

1. Find the **Sign out** button in the top-right corner of any screen.
2. Select it.
3. You return to the sign-in screen.

## Switching to another staff member

The dashboard does not have "switch user". To change who is signed in:

1. The current person selects **Sign out**.
2. The next person signs in with their own email, password, and two-factor code.

## Why every staff member needs their own account

Every action you take — viewing a record, sending a message, flagging a patient — is recorded in the **audit log** against the person who is signed in. The audit log is the clinic's permanent record of who did what.

If you use someone else's login, the audit log shows their name instead of yours. That breaks the clinic's accountability and can cause problems in a clinical review.

:::patient-sees
Nothing — this is staff-internal.
:::

:::watch-out
Never share your login or stay signed in on a shared computer. Always sign out before you step away.
:::

:::tip
If you forget to sign out, the dashboard will eventually end your session — but signing out yourself is the safe habit.
:::
