**What this is for:** Getting into the Focus Vision staff dashboard the first time, and setting up the security you will use at every sign-in.

## What you need

- The staff portal web address (your clinic manager will send this).
- The email address your account was created with.
- A temporary password from your clinic manager.
- An **authenticator app** on your phone — a free app that generates a 6-digit code that changes every 30 seconds (for example Google Authenticator or Authy).

## Steps

1. Open the staff portal link in your browser.
2. Enter your email address and the temporary password, then select **Sign in**.
3. When prompted, set a new password that only you know.
4. The screen shows a QR code. Open your authenticator app and scan it.
5. The app now shows a 6-digit code. Type that code into the dashboard to confirm the link.
6. You are in. The Home screen loads.

This 6-digit code is your **two-factor authentication** (a second security check on top of your password). You will enter a fresh code every time you sign in.

:::patient-sees
Nothing — this is staff-internal.
:::

:::watch-out
Save your authenticator setup carefully. If you lose your phone, you cannot sign in until your clinic manager resets two-factor for you.
:::

:::tip
Sign in once on your usual work computer to confirm everything works before your first shift.
:::
