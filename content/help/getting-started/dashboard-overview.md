**What this is for:** Understanding the three main parts of the staff dashboard so you can find your way around.

## The three parts of the screen

1. **The sidebar** runs down the left edge. It is the dark green menu that takes you to every area of the dashboard.
2. **The top bar** sits across the top of the page. It holds the **Sign out** button on the right.
3. **The main content area** fills the rest of the screen. Whatever you select in the sidebar opens here.

## The Home screen

Home is the first screen you see after signing in. It is your shift starting point — a summary of work waiting for the clinic right now.

- Priority items (flagged patients and check-ins needing review) are listed at the top, most urgent first.
- A live activity feed shows recent events across the clinic — new check-ins, messages, and flags.
- Coloured left borders mark how urgent each item is: red is most urgent, then orange, then yellow.

Select any item to jump straight to it.

:::patient-sees
Nothing — this is staff-internal. The dashboard is the staff side; patients use their own separate app.
:::

:::tip
Start every shift on Home. It tells you what needs attention before anything else.
:::
