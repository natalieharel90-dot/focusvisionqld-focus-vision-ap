**What this is for:** Writing a fresh message and broadcasting it to a chosen cohort of patients.

## How to send

1. Open **Bulk push** and build your cohort filter on the **Compose** tab.
2. Under "What are you sending?", choose **Custom message**.
3. Type a short, clear **title** — patients see this first.
4. Write the **message** in a warm, plain tone from the care team.
5. Optionally attach an image, video, or document with **Add attachment**.
6. Leave the schedule on **Send now**, or pick a later time.
7. Click **Send to N patients** and confirm in the pop-up.

Each recipient gets the message in their own thread, labelled "From Focus Vision team". Any attachment is delivered to every recipient.

## Who can send

Sending is limited to higher-access staff — owners, admins, and clinical leads. Reception-level accounts have view-only access and will see a note asking them to involve an admin or surgeon instead.

:::tip
Keep the title under a sentence. A clear title like "A heads-up about halos at this stage" is easier to act on than a vague one.
:::

:::watch-out
A bulk message cannot be unsent. Read it back, check the recipient count in the preview panel, and confirm carefully before sending.
:::

:::patient-sees
Each patient in the cohort receives a new message in their app thread and a notification, exactly like a direct message from the clinic.
:::
