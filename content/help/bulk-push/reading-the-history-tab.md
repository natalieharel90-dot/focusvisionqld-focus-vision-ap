**What this is for:** Checking what bulk pushes have been sent, who they reached, and how many patients opened them.

## What the History tab shows

The **History** tab lists every bulk push, newest first. Each row shows:

- **Sent / scheduled** — the send time, or the scheduled time with a **Scheduled** badge if it has not fired yet.
- **Sender** — the staff member who created the push.
- **Cohort** — a short summary of the filter, such as "14 LASIK patients · days 3–7".
- **Content** — the title of the message or content sent.
- **Reached** — how many patients received it.
- **Opened** — how many opened it, with the open rate as a percentage.

## Tracking opens

The **open rate** is the share of reached patients who opened the message. Scheduled pushes show a dash for Reached and Opened until they actually fire.

## How to see the detail

1. Open **Bulk push** and click the **History** tab.
2. Click any row to open its detail drawer.
3. Review the content preview, the cohort summary, and the totals.
4. Scroll the **Recipients** list to see each patient, their recovery day, and whether they opened it.

:::tip
A low open rate can mean the timing was off. Compare it with other pushes to learn when your patients are most likely to read messages.
:::

:::watch-out
"Opened" means the patient viewed the message — it does not confirm they read or acted on it. For anything that needs a response, follow up individually.
:::

:::patient-sees
Nothing — the History tab is staff-internal. Opening a message in their app is simply recorded so staff can see the open rate.
:::
