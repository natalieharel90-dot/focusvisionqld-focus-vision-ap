**What this is for:** Sharing a video, article, or FAQ from the content library with a whole cohort at once.

## What gets pushed

Instead of writing a new message, you can push items that already exist in the **content library**. Bulk push only offers items tagged for the **post-op** or **both** audience, since pushes go to patients who are recovering.

The chosen items are composed into a message for each recipient, with the title and link of every item. It lands in their thread like any other message from the clinic.

## How to push content

1. Open **Bulk push** and build your cohort filter on the **Compose** tab.
2. Under "What are you sending?", choose **Existing content**.
3. Tick the library items you want to share.
4. Choose **Send now** or schedule it for later.
5. Click **Send to N patients** and confirm.

You can also choose **Both** to pair a custom message with library items in the same push.

:::tip
If a library item is missing from the list, check its audience tag in the content library — pre-op-only items are deliberately hidden from bulk push.
:::

:::watch-out
Pushing content sends it to every matched patient at once. To share an item with just one person, pin it to their record instead.
:::

:::patient-sees
Each patient receives a message titled "Recommended for you" that lists the shared items with links to open them.
:::
