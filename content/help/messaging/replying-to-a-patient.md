**What this is for:** Sending a reply to a patient from the shared inbox.

## How to reply

1. Open **Messages** from the sidebar.
2. Select the patient's thread from the inbox list.
3. Read the conversation — the most recent message is at the bottom.
4. Type your reply in the compose box at the foot of the thread.
5. Click the **send** button (the paper-plane icon).

Your reply appears in the thread straight away and is delivered to the patient's app.

## Context while you reply

The thread header shows the patient's recovery day, procedure, surgeon, and the result of their last check-in. Use this to tailor your reply. **Open patient** takes you to their full record if you need more detail.

## Attachments and templates

You can attach a file to a reply using the attachment field below the compose box. To reply faster, tap a **quick-reply template** chip to pre-fill the box — see the next article.

## What gets recorded

Every reply you send is audit-logged against the patient with your name and the time. Opening the thread also marks the patient's messages as read.

:::watch-out
The app is for education and adherence support, not diagnosis. Do not tell a patient what condition they have — route clinical advice through the surgeon or optometrist.
:::

:::patient-sees
The patient gets a push notification and sees your reply in their messaging tab as a message from Focus Vision.
:::
