**What this is for:** Using saved message templates to reply to common patient questions quickly and consistently.

## What quick-reply templates are

Quick-reply templates are pre-written messages the clinic has saved for questions that come up often — drop schedules, post-op do's and don'ts, appointment reminders. They appear as a row of **chips** just above the compose box in an open thread.

## How to use a template

1. Open the patient's thread in the shared inbox.
2. Look at the row of template chips above the compose box.
3. Tap the chip that matches the patient's question.
4. The template text fills the compose box — **it is not sent yet**.
5. Edit it to suit the patient, then click send.

Tapping a chip never sends automatically. You always get to personalise and check the message first.

:::tip
Hover over a chip to preview the full template text before you tap it.
:::

:::watch-out
Templates are generic by design. Always edit in the patient's name or specifics so the reply does not feel like a form letter.
:::

:::patient-sees
The patient sees a normal message from Focus Vision — they cannot tell a template was used.
:::

## Where templates come from

The list of templates is managed in **Settings → Message templates**. Adding, editing, or retiring a template there updates the chips for everyone.
