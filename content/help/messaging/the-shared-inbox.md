**What this is for:** Understanding how the staff messaging inbox works — that it is shared, and who can see and reply to patient messages.

## One shared inbox

Focus Vision uses a single **shared inbox**. Every patient message lands in the same place, and **any staff member can reply** — including Reception. There are no private mailboxes.

This means a patient never waits for one specific person. Whoever is free picks up the conversation.

## What you see on the page

The inbox page has two columns:

- **Inbox list** — every conversation thread, newest first, with the patient's name, recovery day, a preview of the last message, and a red dot for unread.
- **Open thread** — the full conversation for the thread you select.

The header shows how many threads are unread and reminds you the inbox is visible to all staff.

## Filtering the list

Use the filter to narrow the list to **All**, **Unread**, **Mine** (threads assigned to you), or **Resolved**.

## Hand-offs between colleagues

Because the inbox is shared, a conversation can pass between staff. When one colleague's reply follows another's, the thread shows a small "added to [name]'s reply" note so the history stays clear.

:::tip
Threads start when the patient sends their first message. Staff cannot open a brand-new thread yet — that is coming in a later release.
:::

:::patient-sees
The patient sees a single conversation with "Focus Vision" — they do not see that different staff members are replying.
:::
