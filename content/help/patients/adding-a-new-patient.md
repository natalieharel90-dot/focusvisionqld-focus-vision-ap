**What this is for:** Creating a new patient and getting them set up for recovery.

## The New patients queue

When a patient signs up through the app, they appear in **New patients** in the sidebar. The queue shows a card per patient with a **setup checklist** — phone verified, procedure template applied, welcome message sent, first appointment booked, and pre-op content assigned. Work through the checklist to activate them.

## Setting up a new patient

To create a patient yourself, use the **Set up new patient** form (the **+ Add patient** button on the Patients screen).

1. Enter the patient's **first name** and **surname**.
2. Enter their **email** address.
3. Add a **phone number** and **date of birth** (phone is optional).
4. Choose the **eye or eyes** being treated and the **surgery date**.
5. Pick a **procedure template** — listed as surgeon and procedure together.
6. Choose the **day hospital** if the surgery is at a partner facility.
7. Select **Create patient & apply template**.

Picking a template automatically applies its default medications and appointments to the new patient.

The patient is created with a temporary password (`welcome-to-focus-vision`). They reset it when they first sign in.

:::patient-sees
The patient can sign in to their app with their email and the temporary password. Once activated, they see their procedure, medications, and appointments ready to go.
:::

:::watch-out
Templates only appear if they exist and are active. If the picker is empty, create one in the **Procedures library** first.
:::
