**What this is for:** Getting from the patient list into one patient's full record.

## Steps

1. In the sidebar, select **Patients**.
2. Find the patient. Type a name, email, or procedure into the search box if the list is long.
3. Select the patient's row.
4. Their detail page opens, with their full record.

To go back, select **← Patients** at the top of the detail page.

## Other ways in

You can also reach a patient's record from:

- The **Home** screen — select a flagged patient or activity item.
- The **New patients** queue — select **View patient record**.
- The **Triage** queue — select the patient on a check-in or flag.

:::patient-sees
The patient is not told when staff open their record. But every time you open it, an entry is written to the audit log with your name, the date, and the time.
:::

:::watch-out
Only open records for a clinical reason. Opening a record is an audited action — casual browsing is not appropriate and is visible in the audit log.
:::
