**What this is for:** Reading the **Patients** screen at a glance — every patient in recovery, one per row.

## Top of the screen

- A short summary shows how many patients are **active** and how many are **new this week**.
- Four cards show clinic numbers: active recoveries, pending check-ins, flagged for review, and 7-day medication adherence (how reliably doses are taken).
- A search box filters the list by name, email, or procedure.
- An **+ Add patient** button starts a new patient.

## The columns

- **Patient** — name and a coloured initials circle.
- **Procedure** — the procedure type and which eye or eyes, for example "LASIK · both eyes".
- **Day** — how many days since surgery, shown as "Day 5". This is the patient's recovery day.
- **Last check-in** — when they last completed a daily check-in: "Today", "Yesterday", a date, or "Pending".
- **Adherence** — the percentage of medication doses taken on time over the last 7 days.
- **Status** — a coloured label summarising the patient right now.

## Status labels

- **On track** (green) — nothing needs attention.
- **Awaiting check-in** or **Missed doses** (amber) — worth a look.
- **Pain reported** or **Flagged for review** (red) — needs attention.
- **Discharged** (grey) — no longer in active recovery.

:::patient-sees
Nothing — this is staff-internal. These columns summarise data the patient enters in their own app.
:::

:::tip
Scan the **Status** column first. Red labels are where your attention is needed.
:::
