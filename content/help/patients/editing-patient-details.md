**What this is for:** Correcting or updating a patient's personal and contact details on their record.

## Steps

1. Open the patient's record (see [Opening a patient's record](/help/patients/opening-a-patient-record)).
2. In the **Patient details** panel, select **Edit**.
3. Update any field — first name, surname, email, phone, date of birth, or allergies.
4. Enter allergies as a comma-separated list, for example "Penicillin, Latex".
5. Select **Save details**.

The change is recorded in the audit log with your name and the old and new values.

## A note on phone numbers

If you change the patient's **phone number**, it is marked **unverified** until the patient re-confirms it themselves by SMS. This is a safety check — it makes sure the clinic only ever has a confirmed, working number for that patient.

:::patient-sees
Updated details flow through to the patient's app. If you change the phone number, the patient is asked to re-confirm it by SMS before it is used again.
:::

:::watch-out
Always double-check spelling of the email address. The patient signs in with it, so a typo can lock them out of their app.
:::

:::tip
For surgical details — surgeon, procedure type, surgery date — use the **Procedures** panel, not Patient details.
:::
