**What this is for:** Surfacing one specific library item in a single patient's app, beyond what their procedure tags already show.

## What pinning does

**Pinning** attaches a content library item to one individual patient so it appears prominently in their app. Use it when a patient needs a particular video or article that their procedure and recovery stage would not surface on their own — for example, an extra aftercare FAQ for a patient who keeps asking the same question.

Pinning does not change the library item itself. The same item can be pinned to many patients independently.

## How to pin an item

1. Open the patient's record from the patient list.
2. Go to the **Content** or **Education** section of their detail page.
3. Select **Pin content**.
4. Search the content library and choose the item you want.
5. Confirm — the item now shows in that patient's app.

To remove it later, open the same section and unpin the item.

:::tip
Pinning is best for one-off, patient-specific needs. If a whole group needs the same item, push it to a cohort instead — it is faster than pinning one by one.
:::

:::watch-out
Pinned content does not expire on its own. Unpin items that are no longer relevant so the patient's app stays focused.
:::

:::patient-sees
The pinned item appears near the top of the patient's learning area, marked so it stands out from their standard recovery content.
:::
