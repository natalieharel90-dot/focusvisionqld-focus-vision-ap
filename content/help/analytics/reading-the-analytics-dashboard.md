**What this is for:** Giving clinic leaders a clear, clinic-wide view of how recoveries are going — without ever opening an individual patient record.

## What the dashboard shows

The analytics dashboard at **Practice analytics** is aggregated only. It never shows patient names or individual records — just clinic-wide numbers.

The page has three layers:

1. **Stat cards** — eight quick-view numbers across the top, such as total patients, new patients, app active rate, check-in completion, medication adherence, median staff response, and red alert rate.
2. **Charts** — the recovery progress curve, adherence by procedure, zone breakdown, most-reported symptoms, patient feedback, check-in completion, and a procedure-by-zone map.
3. **Surgeon performance** — a table comparing surgeons on patient count, adherence, rating, and flag rate.

## Filtering and refreshing

1. Set a **From** and **To** date at the top of the page.
2. Use the **Procedures** pills to narrow to one or more procedure types.
3. Click **Apply** to update every chart, or **Reset** to clear filters.

:::tip
The data is pre-computed and refreshed nightly. Click **Refresh data** to pull the latest figures on demand.
:::

:::watch-out
A green or red arrow under a stat card compares the current range to the previous equal-length range — not to a fixed target.
:::

Nothing — this is staff-internal.
:::
