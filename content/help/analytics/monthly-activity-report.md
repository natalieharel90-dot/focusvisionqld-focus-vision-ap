**What this is for:** Producing a one-page, printable summary of a single calendar month — onboarding, check-ins, adherence, messaging, and flags.

## Who can generate it

Reports are tier-restricted. Clinic owners, admins, and clinical staff can generate and download reports. Reception cannot.

## Generating the report

1. Open **Reports** from the sidebar.
2. Select the **Monthly clinic activity** tab.
3. Choose a **Month** (leave it blank to use the previous full month).
4. Decide whether to tick **Include patient identifiers** — see the caution below.
5. Click **Generate report**.
6. Open the new entry under **Previously generated** to view the finished page.

## What the report contains

- **Patients onboarded** — count and list, with procedure and surgery date.
- **Check-ins completed** — completed versus expected, plus a zone breakdown.
- **Medication adherence** — percentage of scheduled doses taken.
- **Messages** — patient and staff message counts, and median response time.
- **Manual flags** — count by level and average time to resolution.

:::watch-out
**Include patient identifiers** is off by default. Leave it off unless you have a clear reason — reports then use initials only and carry no personally identifiable information (PII).
:::

:::tip
Turn on **Schedule monthly** to have this report generated automatically on the 1st of each month.
:::
