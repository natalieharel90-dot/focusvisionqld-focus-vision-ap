**What this is for:** Clearing a triage card once you have dealt with a flagged patient, so the queue stays accurate for the whole team.

## When to resolve

Resolve a card once you have done whatever the flag called for — reviewed a yellow card's details, contacted an orange patient, or actioned a red alert. Resolving means "no further staff action needed".

## How to resolve a card

1. Open the **triage queue** from the sidebar.
2. Find the patient's card in the Red, Orange, or Yellow section.
3. Review the **Reported** details, and use **Message** or **Open** if you need to act first.
4. Once handled, click **Resolve** on the card.

The card moves to the **Recently resolved (today)** list at the bottom, showing your name and the time.

## What resolving records

Resolving is audit-logged with your name, the time, and the patient. For a check-in, this marks it reviewed; for a manual flag, this closes the flag.

:::watch-out
Resolving a card does not message the patient. If the patient still needs to hear from the clinic, send a message before you resolve.
:::

:::tip
The **Resolved today** stat card is a quick way for the team to see how much triage work has already been cleared.
:::

:::patient-sees
Nothing — resolving is staff-internal. The patient's zone screen does not change when you resolve their card.
:::
