**What this is for:** Keeping the list of people who work at Focus Vision up to date, so surgeon dropdowns across the dashboard show the right names.

## Where it lives

Staff are managed in the **Staff** section of **Settings → Clinic & Staff**.

## Adding a staff member

1. Open **Settings** and select the **Clinic & Staff** tab.
2. In the **Staff** section, click **Add staff**.
3. Enter the person's name, choose a **role** (surgeon, optometrist, nurse, or reception), and add their details.
4. Save. New staff appear with an "Invited · sign-in not set up" badge until they complete first sign-in.

## Surgeon extras

Surgeons appear as options in every surgeon dropdown on the dashboard. Each surgeon can also:

- Have a **bio** shown to patients.
- Record a **welcome message** transcript for the Surgeon Spotlight screen.
- Upload an optional **welcome video** — use **Choose video**, then **Upload**.

## Removing someone

1. Find the staff member in the list.
2. Click **Remove from roster**, then confirm — this **deactivates** them, keeping their history.
3. Deactivated staff can be **Reactivated** anytime, or **Deleted permanently** if they have no clinical or audit records.

:::watch-out
Deactivate rather than delete. Deleting is blocked once a staff member has audit history, which protects the 7-year record.
:::

:::patient-sees
Surgeon names, bios, welcome messages, and videos appear in the patient app. A deactivated surgeon stays attached to past patients but is hidden from new selections.
:::
