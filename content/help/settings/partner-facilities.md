**What this is for:** Maintaining the list of day-surgery hospitals where Focus Vision procedures are performed, so patients can name where they had surgery when they give feedback.

## Where it lives

Partner facilities are managed under **Settings → Day-surgery partners**. They also appear in the **Day-surgery partner facilities** section of the **Clinic & Staff** tab.

A **day hospital** is an outside facility where a procedure happens and the patient goes home the same day.

## Adding a facility

1. Open **Settings** and select the **Day-surgery partners** tab.
2. Click **Add facility**.
3. Enter the facility **name** and a **liaison email** — the contact person at that facility.
4. Save.

## Editing or removing a facility

1. Find the facility in the list.
2. Click **Edit** to change its name, liaison email, or active status.
3. To stop offering it, set it **inactive**, or click **Remove** and confirm to delete it.

:::tip
Set a facility to inactive rather than removing it if you may use it again — this keeps it out of the patient feedback list without losing the record.
:::

:::patient-sees
Active partner facilities appear as feedback options in the patient app, so patients can say which facility they attended. Inactive facilities are hidden.
:::
