**What this is for:** Turning optional patient-app features on or off — both as a clinic-wide default and for individual patients.

## Where it lives

Feature toggles are under **Settings → Patient app features**.

## The optional features

- **Surgeon Spotlight video** — a personal welcome video on the patient's home screen.
- **Eye photo prompt** — asks the patient to add an eye photo with their daily check-in.
- **Daily check-in nudge** — a reminder if the patient hasn't checked in by the nudge time.
- **Lock-screen widget offer** — offers the next-dose widget in patient settings.
- **Feedback tile** — shows the Feedback tile on the home screen.
- **Pre-op tile** — shows the Before-your-surgery tile until the surgery date.
- **Bonus theme pack** — lets the patient discover a pack of extra app themes.

## Changing a clinic-wide default

1. Open **Settings** and select the **Patient app features** tab.
2. Click the **ON / OFF** pill next to a feature to flip its default.
3. For the check-in nudge, also pick a **nudge time** and click **Save time**.

:::watch-out
Changing a default only affects patients onboarded **after** the change. Existing patients keep their current state.
:::

## Overriding for one patient

1. Click **Manage overrides** next to a feature, or use the patient's detail page.
2. Set that patient's toggle on or off.

:::tip
Surgeon Spotlight only plays if that patient's surgeon has uploaded a welcome video — the page shows how many surgeons have one.
:::

:::patient-sees
Each enabled feature changes the patient app — a video on home, a photo step in check-in, a reminder, or an extra tile.
:::
