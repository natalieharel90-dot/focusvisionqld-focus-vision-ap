**What this is for:** Controlling the buttons a patient sees on their Contact screen — call the clinic, send a message, view directions, and the after-hours emergency notice.

## Where it lives

Contact options live under **Settings → Contact screen**. The page shows an editor on the left and a **live patient preview** on the right.

## Adding or editing an option

1. Open **Settings** and select the **Contact screen** tab.
2. Click **Add contact option**, or the edit icon on an existing one.
3. Set the **label**, **subtitle**, **icon**, and what the option does.
4. Save — the preview updates immediately.

## Reordering and enabling

1. Use the **up** and **down** arrows to change the order patients see.
2. Use the toggle to **enable** or **disable** an option without deleting it.
3. Options marked with a lock are **required** and cannot be deleted.

## The after-hours emergency notice

Below the contact options is the **After-hours emergency notice**. Set the **after-hours phone**, a **display label**, and the **message text**, then click **Save notice**.

:::watch-out
The after-hours notice always shows in red at the bottom of the patient's Contact screen. Keep the number current — anxious patients rely on it outside clinic hours.
:::

:::patient-sees
Enabled contact options appear, in order, on the patient's Contact screen. Disabled options vanish; the after-hours notice always stays visible.
:::
