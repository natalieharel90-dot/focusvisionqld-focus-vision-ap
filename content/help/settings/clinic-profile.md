**What this is for:** Keeping the clinic's core details — name, address, phone, opening hours, and the after-hours emergency number — correct everywhere they appear in the patient app.

## Where it lives

The clinic profile sits under **Settings → Clinic & Staff**, in the **Clinic details** and **Opening hours** sections at the top of the page.

## Editing clinic details

1. Open **Settings** and select the **Clinic & Staff** tab.
2. Update the **Clinic name**, **ABN** (Australian Business Number), **Address**, **Main phone**, **Email**, or **Website** as needed.
3. Click **Save clinic profile** at the bottom of the form.

The **Timezone** field is fixed and cannot be edited — it keeps medication reminders and check-in times correct for patients.

## Setting opening hours

1. Scroll to the **Opening hours** section.
2. Tick or untick the **Open** box for each weekday.
3. Set the **start** and **end** time for each open day.
4. Click **Save clinic profile**.

## The after-hours emergency number

The after-hours phone number is **not** edited here. It lives under **Settings → Contact screen**, in the **After-hours emergency notice** section.

:::watch-out
The after-hours number is shown to anxious patients outside clinic hours. Check it after any phone-system change.
:::

:::patient-sees
The clinic name, address, opening hours, and after-hours notice appear on the patient's Contact screen and update as soon as you save.
:::
