**What this is for:** Knowing what a brand-new patient is shown the first time they open the app, so you can reassure them it's a normal welcome step.

## When the tour appears

The first time an activated patient opens the home screen, a full-screen welcome tour plays over the top. It runs once. After the patient finishes or skips it, it does not appear again.

## The five slides

The tour is a five-slide carousel — each slide is an icon, a title, and one short line of copy:

1. **Welcome to Focus Vision** — a warm intro; the tour takes about 30 seconds
2. **Your daily check-in** — a quick daily check helps spot anything early
3. **Medication reminders** — where the eye-drop schedule lives, and that doses can be marked or snoozed
4. **Message your care team** — patients can reach the clinic anytime
5. **You're all set** — everything is ready to go

The patient taps the slide (or a progress dot) to move forward, and there is a **Skip** button to exit early at any point.

## Replaying it

Patients can see the tour again anytime from **Settings → Accessibility → Replay app tour**.

:::patient-sees
The tour follows the patient's theme and dark mode, so it doesn't look jarring. It is purely a friendly welcome — it collects no information and skipping it has no downside.
:::

:::tip
If a patient is unsure how the app works, point them to Settings to replay the tour. It is the fastest 30-second refresher you can offer over the phone.
:::
