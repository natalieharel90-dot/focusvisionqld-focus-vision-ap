**What this is for:** Getting the staff dashboard working again when a page looks broken, shows blank areas, or will not load data.

Most display problems are caused by the browser or the internet connection, not by your patient data. Work through the quick checks below before reporting a fault.

## Things to try, in order

1. Check your internet connection by opening any other website.
2. Refresh the page — press the reload button or **Ctrl+R** (Windows) or **Cmd+R** (Mac).
3. If parts of the screen are still missing, do a hard refresh with **Ctrl+Shift+R** or **Cmd+Shift+R**.
4. Sign out and sign back in to get a fresh session.
5. Close the browser completely and open it again.
6. Try a different browser, such as Chrome or Edge, to see if the problem follows you.
7. Restart your computer if the screen is still wrong.

:::tip
A hard refresh clears the page's saved files and reloads everything fresh. It fixes most "half-loaded" or "stuck old version" problems.
:::

:::watch-out
Do not assume data is lost just because a page is blank. The patient records are stored safely on the server — a blank screen is almost always a display problem on your device.
:::

If the problem continues after all of these steps, capture what is going wrong before reporting it. Note the page you were on, the time, and any error message. The browser developer console (press **F12**) shows the technical error — see the article below for how to read it.
